Midnights Map Style

Brynn Patrello, November 12, 2024

I am a huge swiftie, so when tasked with creating a map style inspired by an aesthetic of my choice, I thought to Swift's distinct aesthetics created through each album era. Particularly, her Grammy-winning album Midnights created the foundation for which this map was inspired. I explain many of my design choices through a scroll map exploring parameters like form, color, typography, and texture that were considered in its creation.
