Risky Business

Brynn Patrello, October 7, 2024

    Travel provides many opportunities for people to learn about different cultures, see life from a new perspective, and expand their worldview. However, when not well-informed, it can present some dangers to the traveler- which is why resources like Travel Advisories exist, to minimize the risk of traveling for people.
    Travel Advisories incorporate two more concepts that interest me: data transparency/communication and geopolitical relations. Travel Advisories aim to inform travelers of risks, but can sometimes over-generalize, lack context, and provide only one administrative perspective. Geopolitical relations also impact Travel Advisories based on one governmentâ€™s view on another country.
    These interests inspired this StoryMap project, in partial fulfillment of the requirements for GEOG 572: Graphic Design in Cartography course at UW-Madison. Risky Business aims to inform the audience about what travel advisories are, promote critical thinking of travel advisories, and overall encourage safe travel practice.
